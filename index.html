UBC Mars Colony | Project Helios

Project Helios is a currently a sub sector of UBC's student run Mars Colony design team. Driven by the potential of outer space, Helios has the initiative of providing sustainable energy to Earth by extracting Helium-3 from the Moon.

Team

Our Lunar Extraction team is made up of a diverse group of young motivated engineers. The motto is to never stop learning and reaching as we continue to pursue this dream of making Helios come to life.

Energy Source of Tomorrow?

Helium-3 (He3) is an element that has the potential to be used as a fuel for immense and unimaginable amounts of power in future nuclear fusion power plants. There is very little helium-3 available on the Earth. However, there are thought to be significant supplies on the Moon.

Extraction

With every will, there must be a way. The project Helios team is trying to implement a strategy to mine, process and store this precious element so it can be sent back to our planet for use in powerplants.
